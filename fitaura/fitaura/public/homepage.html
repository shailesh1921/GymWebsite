The Process

Systematic Progression

01

Execute & Record

Complete your prescribed session. Log precise weights, reps, and—crucially—your Rate of Perceived Exertion (RPE). Accurate data is the fuel for accurate coaching.

02

Quantify Recovery

Biofeedback matters. Before every session, input sleep quality, soreness, and stress levels. Calibra weighs these variables to determine your daily readiness.

03

Receive Adaptation

Instantaneously, your plan evolves. Volume may drop to manage fatigue, or intensity may spike to capitalize on a peak performance window.

04

Understand the Logic

Review the generated explanation. Understand the specific coaching principle applied to your session. Become an informed athlete.
